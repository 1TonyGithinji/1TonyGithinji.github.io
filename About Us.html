>This site is designed to give detailed information to policy makers about issues that affect the quality of the road infrastructure such as potholes, lack of/or fading road markings, as well as damaged equipment such as road rails, vandalized road signs.
>Once you make a report, an email will be sent to the relevant officers and a reference number will be created automatically. A message acknowledging receipt of your report will be sent to you via your email and/or your phone or as a notification on your app. The message will include the reference number.
